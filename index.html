About Haunted Hacks

Haunted Hacks is a twenty-four hour hackathon for high school students to explore technology's role in social innovation in a slightly *sweeter* way than normal. Come in your costumes for a night of fun as you come up with creative ways to make our planet a little less scary.

About The Young Hackers

Based in NYC, we are an organization of high school students that exists to empower the next generation of programmers. Our goal is to bring together students from diverse backgrounds who are interested in technology and programming to learn, connect and build.

Frequently Asked Questions


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event in which teams spend the weekend building something awesome (and then demo it). Haunted Hacks is open to anyone, from the absolute beginner to the professional hacker. Check out Dave Fontenot's post on "What is a hackathon?"


What if I don't know how to code?

There is absolutely nothing wrong with that! Hackathons provide an awesome opportunity for people to learn how to code. Even if you have never programmed before in your life, we will help you get started with the basics of programming and hopefully by the end of the hackathon, you will leave feeling super proud of yourself!


What should I bring? How much does this event cost?

You should bring your laptop (we won't provide computers) and charger, school ID, phone and charger, and power strips (recommended). Food will be provided! This event costs absolutely nothing!


Who can attend and how do the teams work?

Any students in high school can attend! You do not have to be from the US. If you need help convincing your parents, contact us and we'll talk to them about safety measures here at Haunted Hacks. You can work individually or with a team (with a suggested maximum of 4 hackers). For more information, check out our rules.


I know a company that wants to sponsor, what should I tell them?

Great! Tell them to contact us at usyounghackers@gmail.com and we'll get back to them lazer fast!


Can I be a mentor at Haunted Hacks?

Definitely! Mentor registration will be out a bit earlier than standard registration, so sit tight!


I have another question! How should I ask?

We love to answer questions! Contact us at usyounghackers@gmail.com and we'll respond within 24 hours.
